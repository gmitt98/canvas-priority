How to get your Canvas access token

  1. Log into Canvas at your school's website
  2. Click your Account menu (top-left avatar)
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Scroll to Approved Integrations
  5. Click + New Access Token
  6. Enter a purpose (e.g., "Canvas Priority Extension")
  7. Click Generate Token
  8. Copy the token and paste it above

🔒 Keep your token private. It grants access to your Canvas account.
